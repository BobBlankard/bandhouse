BandHouse Support

Need help with the app or have questions about upcoming features? We’re here to keep your local music scene thriving.

Contact Us

Email our support team anytime at BandHouseHelp@gmail.com.

Please include your device model, iOS version, and a brief description of the issue (screenshots help!). We aim to respond within 24 hours.

Email BandHouse Support

Quick Help

Forgot your password? Use “Forgot Password” on the login screen to reset via email.

Issues posting a flyer? Make sure you’ve granted Photos access and selected a valid venue address from autocomplete.

Location not updating? Check iOS Settings → Privacy → Location Services → BandHouse and set it to “While Using the App.”

Report Content

If you see inappropriate flyers or profiles, tap the ••• menu on the item and choose “Report”, or click the flag icon located on the bottom right of posts on feed. Send us an email if urgent—we actively monitor all reports to keep the community safe.
