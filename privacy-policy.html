Privacy Policy for BandHouse

Last Updated: October 22, 2025

Introduction

BandHouse ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Name, email address, password
  • Profile Information: User type (Viewer, Band, Host), profile picture (optional)
  • Event Content: Event flyers, venue information, dates, times, ticket links
  • Social Links: Instagram handles and Spotify links (optional)

Information We Collect Automatically

  • Location Data: We collect your device's location to show nearby events and calculate distances
  • Usage Data: App interactions, features used, events viewed
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers
  • Analytics: Firebase Analytics data to improve app performance

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the BandHouse service
  • Show you nearby events based on your location
  • Enable you to post and discover music events
  • Connect bands with venues and music fans
  • Send notifications about events (with your permission)
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Prevent fraud and abuse
  • Comply with legal obligations

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We Share Data With:

  • Google Maps/Places: To display maps and autocomplete addresses
  • Firebase (Google): For authentication, database, and analytics
  • Other Users: Your posted events and public profile information are visible to other app users
  • Service Providers: Third-party services that help us operate the app

We Do NOT:

  • Sell your personal information to third parties
  • Share your email or password with anyone
  • Use your location data for purposes other than showing nearby events

Your Rights and Choices

You Can:

  • Access: View all your posted events in your profile
  • Edit: Update or delete your posted events
  • Delete Account: Contact us to delete your account and data
  • Opt-Out: Disable location services (may limit app functionality)
  • Export: Request a copy of your data

Location Services

  • You can enable/disable location access in iOS Settings
  • Without location access, distance filtering will not work
  • We only access your location when you use the app (not in background)

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures including:

  • Firebase Authentication with encrypted passwords
  • Secure HTTPS connections for all data transmission
  • Firebase App Check to prevent abuse
  • Regular security updates

Children's Privacy

BandHouse is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe we have collected data from a child, please contact us immediately.

Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active
  • Posted Events: Retained until you delete them or your account
  • Analytics: Anonymized analytics data may be retained indefinitely
  • Deleted Data: Removed within 30 days of account deletion

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by:

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Sending an in-app notification
  • Posting a notice in the app

Content Moderation

BandHouse allows users to post event flyers and other content. We take user safety seriously:

  • Users can report inappropriate content using the in-app report button
  • We review all reports and remove content that violates our guidelines
  • Repeat violators may have their accounts suspended or terminated
  • We cooperate with law enforcement when required by law

  • Right of access to your data

  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing
